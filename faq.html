Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for travelers browsing stays and owners listing their property.

For Travelers

Do I book directly through OverNightStays?

No. We're a local directory, not a booking agent. Every listing shows the owner's phone number or WhatsApp so you can contact them directly, agree on dates and pricing, and arrange your stay without any middleman fees.

Is it free to browse listings?

Yes, browsing B&Bs, restaurants, events, and attractions on the Guide is completely free for travelers — no account required.

How do I know a listing is accurate and up to date?

Property owners manage their own listings from their dashboard, so pricing, photos, and availability are kept current by the people who run the property. We recommend confirming exact availability and pricing directly with the owner before finalizing plans.

Can I filter listings by price or amenities?

Yes. On the Browse Stays page you can sort by price, set a maximum price, and filter by amenities like WiFi, breakfast, and parking.

What if a property doesn't respond to me?

Since arrangements happen directly with owners, response times can vary. If a property isn't responding, try their alternate contact method on the listing, or reach out to us and we'll follow up on our end.

For B&B Owners

How much does it cost to list my property?

Listing your B&B is free, with zero commission on bookings. You keep full control of pricing and guest communication.

How do I add my property?

Sign up for an owner account, then add your listing from your dashboard — including your main photo, gallery, description, amenities, and individual rooms with their own pricing.

Does my listing appear immediately?

New listings are reviewed before they go live to keep the directory trustworthy for travelers. Once approved, your property appears in Browse Stays and can be found via search and filters.

Can I manage multiple rooms with different prices?

Yes. The Rooms & Pricing manager on your listing lets you add separate room types, each with its own price, bed count, guest capacity, description, and photos.

How do I update or remove a listing?

From your dashboard, open the listing you want to change and use the edit page to update details, swap the main image, or manage your gallery and rooms at any time.

Who handles payments and bookings?

You do. OverNightStays connects you with travelers, but all booking arrangements, deposits, and payments happen directly between you and your guests.
