Who We Are

Stay Carletonville is more than just a booking platform – we're your local guides, born and raised in this beautiful town. We understand the rhythm of our community, the stories behind every corner, and the warmth that makes Carletonville special.

Our team consists of local entrepreneurs, hospitality enthusiasts, and community advocates who are dedicated to showcasing the best of what our town has to offer while supporting the businesses that make our community thrive.
